Hotel Royal Continental

The Royal Continental is located on Naples’ waterfront. It’s easy to lose yourself between the sea and the symbols of the city: opposite the hotel, you’ll see the legendary Castel dell‘Ovo and the Borgo Marinari, just a few minutes walk away is the Piazza del Plebiscito and a little further, the Maschio Angioino.

The hotel is located in the centre of the large pedestrianised island of the promenade, while still being well-connected to the rest of the city.

Hotel's surroundings

Art and culture

Art and culture are in every corner of the city, but we’d like to suggest a few starting points, such as the PAN, which hosts major contemporary art exhibitions, or the National Archaeological Museum. If you’re looking for something a little different, we recommend the tour of the art stations of metro line 1; each station has its own character, colours and theme.

Shopping

Just a few minutes away from the hotel, you’ll find two different shopping areas: Chiaia, with small prestigious boutiques and top fashion labels; and via Toledo, the street with the major international chains.
Both are within short walking distance from the hotel.

Business

The Royal Continental hotel is in an ideal location for business. Not only does it offer modern and functional meeting rooms and spaces, it is also just a few minutes from major business spots, while allowing you to enjoy a scenic location. The offices of the Region of Campania are just a 2-minute walk away, and the Centro Direzionale business district is a quarter of an hour’s drive.

Nightlife

The hotel is one of the top venues of the city, with its brunches and cocktails at the Roof, rooftop restaurant, Lounge Bar and Bistro.

Just a few steps away are the seafront, Borgo Marinari and the Chiaia district, all perfect after-dinner spots: an abundance of restaurants, small pubs and characteristic bars.

How to reach the Hotel

By plane

From Napoli Capodichino airport take the Alibus busline and get off at Piazza Municipio.
Continue on foot to via Acton and take bus 154; get off at the stop for Saint Lucia, and the hotel is just a few steps away.

By train

From Napoli Centrale railway station, take Metro Line 1 (Garibaldi, just outside the station), going towards Dante, and get off at Toledo.
From there, you can take a taxi or enjoy a pleasant walk along Via Toledo and through Piazza del Plebiscito.

By car

From the motorway, drive towards Piazza Municipio/Porto, continue on Via Acton, cross the Tunnel della Vittoria and follow Via Morelli. After 100m turn left into Piazza Vittoria.
Turn left to stay in the square and then turn left into via Arcoleo. Just before the entrance to the Tunnel della Vittoria, turn right into via Chiatamone. At number 54, turn right into via Dumas and at the end of the road turn left into the garage of the hotel.
